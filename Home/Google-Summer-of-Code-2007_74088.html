RVM : Google Summer of Code 2007

Created by Ian Rogers, last modified by Ian Rogers on Mar 04, 2008

Great news! The Jikes RVM is a mentoring organization for the Google Summer of Code (SoC) 2007! With mentors in both hemispheres and around the globe, the Jikes RVM is the project to apply to for thorough mentoring support. Applying to be a student with the Jikes RVM not only gives you an opportunity to improve and demonstrate your programming skills, you get paid and you get to do work that can contribute to project work or research you are undertaking.

The deadline for creating your project proposals is very near, March 26th! To find out more about submitting a project proposal visit our project ideas page. We have many project ideas of our own, but we also welcome new projects that focus on your interests.
